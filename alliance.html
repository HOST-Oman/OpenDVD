Alliance  1.0.6
Share files and communicate privately and securely

Alliance is a private and secure place to share files and communicate with people you know where users create their own networks. You can either start a new network or get invited into an existing network by another Alliance user.

Chat with your friends, family and co-workers in a secure and private way. Share files securely and at high speeds - file transfers use BitTorrent-like technology, meaning that they can be downloaded from multiple sources and uploaded to others at the same time. Search for files in your network using the decentralized high-speed search engine.

Encryption is an important part of Alliance. Robust encryption has been implemented and is available as an experimental option in Alliance. By default Alliance runs on a very simplistic encryption algorithm. Even with the simple encryption Alliance is more secure than normal P2P networks since you only connect to your trusted friends.
